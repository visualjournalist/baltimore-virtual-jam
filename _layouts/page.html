The Baltimore Old Time Jam is a bi-weekly session open to Old-Time musicians of all skill levels and backgrounds with the intention of creating a welcoming, communal and joyful space for making music together. Hosted by Ken & Brad Kolodner every other Tuesday night from 7-10pm at R House in the Remington neighborhood of Baltimore City. The first hour of the jam is a "slow jam" in which common tunes are played at a moderate tempo so anyone can join in. At 8pm, the tempo picks up for the "fast jam." We hope you can join us! When the pandemic hit in 2020, the jam moved online.

You can add tunes to the binder for easy access to the tunes you're working on.
